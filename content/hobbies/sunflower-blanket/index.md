## The Vision & Blueprint

The goal of this project is to create a textured throw blanket using worsted weight yarn. Instead of traditional stripes, it’s built out of modular motifs that form a sunflower bouquet patchwork.

### Color Palette
* **Petals:** Mustard / Goldenrod Yellow
* **Centers:** Espresso / Dark Brown
* **Background & Border:** Sage Green and Cream


## Materials & Specs

* **Yarn:** Worsted weight acrylic (for durability and easy washing)
* **Hook Size:** 5.0 mm (H-8) to keep the stitches relatively tight but soft
* **Final Dimensions:** Target is 50" x 60" (roughly 80 individual square motifs)
